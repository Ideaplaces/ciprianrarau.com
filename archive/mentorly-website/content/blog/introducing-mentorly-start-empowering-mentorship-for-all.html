Today, we are thrilled to announce the launch of a new pricing tier for Mentorly: Mentorly Start! This exciting addition to our lineup is designed to make mentorship accessible to organizations of all sizes, enabling them to foster meaningful connections and empower their members to reach new heights.

At Mentorly, we firmly believe that pricing is not just a number to be set and forgotten about; it is an exercise in understanding our customers and the needs of our market. When we launched Mentorly in the summer of 2020, we mistakenly assumed that only programs over one hundred of users would be interested in adopting mentorship technology. You’ve pointed us in the right direction since then! You’ve taught us that regardless of program size, and getting started with the right tools is important for smaller programs.

With Mentorly Start, we aim to remove any barriers preventing organizations from experiencing the transformative power of mentorship. For a budget-friendly price of only $300 per month, Mentorly Start offers 50 users (mentors and mentees), giving your organization the opportunity to build valuable relationships and foster growth for your community. But that’s not all—Mentorly Start also includes one program and one program manager seat to support the efficient management and smooth operation of your mentorship program.

We are thrilled to share that we already have a handful of clients who have embraced Mentorly Start, and their feedback has been nothing short of fantastic. These organizations are experiencing the positive impact of mentorship firsthand, witnessing the growth and development of their members, and creating a thriving ecosystem of support and guidance. It is exciting to see our vision come to life and to witness the transformative power of mentorship reaching organizations of all sizes.

If you’re intrigued and would like to learn more about Mentorly Start, we encourage you to book a demo with our team. Our dedicated experts will be more than happy to guide you through the platform, showcase the features and benefits of Mentorly Start, and answer any questions you may have. Together, we can unlock the full potential of mentorship within your organization and help you create a community that thrives on connection and growth.

Thank you for being a part of the Mentorly journey. Your support and feedback have been invaluable in shaping our platform and our vision. We remain committed to listening to you, our customers and users, and evolving to not only meet your needs, but build great technology to enable you to build programs that you dream of.

Let’s embark on this exciting new chapter together with Mentorly Start, where mentorship knows no bounds.

Wishing you all the best on your mentoring endeavors!

The Mentorly Team
